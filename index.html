I am Shivangi Khandelwal

I am a professional Anchor, Emcee, and Actor based in Delhi & Mumbai, bringing over 6 years of stage experience and 5,000+ events to every performance.

I have had the privilege of hosting prestigious award shows, corporate summits, government events, luxury brand launches, exhibitions, destination weddings, fashion shows, and grand entertainment events. At the Critics India Awards and IBJA, I shared the stage with Arbaaz Khan, Sonali Bendre, Milind Soman, Neha Dhupia, Vikrant Massey, Amyra Dastur, and many more celebrated personalities.

Brands including Microsoft, Hyundai, Qualcomm, Tata Power, Bajaj Allianz, Shangri-La Hotels, Hindware, IHFF, and over 80 leading organisations have trusted me to anchor their most important occasions.

My hosting style is deeply interactive and audience-centric — creative games, live interactions, spontaneous moments, and high-energy entertainment woven into every event. From formal corporate gatherings to joyous wedding celebrations, I adapt effortlessly to every stage and every audience.
